I developed an iOS app called MedMinder using Swift. MedMinder allows users to store and manage their medication information. The app enables users to update their medication list, helping them keep track of what medications they've taken in the past, including dosages, times, and duration. Designed with responsiveness in mind, MedMinder ensures an optimal user experience on all iOS devices.
